# Customer Churn Reduction Strategy

> Business Analyst Portfolio Project


## Project Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end Business Analysis approach for reducing customer churn in a telecommunications company (AlphaCom). The project covers business problem identification, stakeholder analysis, current-state assessment, root cause analysis, future-state process design, business requirements documentation, Agile user stories, validation artifacts, and interactive business dashboards.

The objective is to support proactive customer retention through structured business analysis and data-driven decision-making.


## Business Problem

AlphaCom is experiencing increasing customer churn, resulting in customer attrition and revenue loss. The organization requires a structured business solution to identify high-risk customers and support proactive retention initiatives.
